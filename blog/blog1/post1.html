Food

The first word that comes ot my head when thinking of the food at Barrow's is exceptional. They have taking a classic fried food dish and have not done anything fancy to it, but rather they have perfected it. They way that they prepare their catfiah is with a classic breading and lightly seasoned. The cruch on the batter and the light flaky meat of the fish make my mouth water just htinking of it. Potatoe salad is served along side the catfish, and the creamy potatoe salad nicely offsets the chrispy bites of catfish. Although I do not know why one would want to stray away from the catfish Barrows does have other options. They also have fried osyters and shrimp with a varity of sides and starters. I have never vertured away from the catfish, but I have no doubt that all the other items on the menu are outstanding.

Service

The service at Barrow's is second to none. Upon walking in you are greated by a highly hospitable host that will seat you at a table. The host when I visited was the nicest man that I have ever meet. He would periodidocally check on us to make sure we were well taking care of. The waitress that took care of me wass also very great; she was alwaalwas checking in to make sure that we had everything that we needed and that the food was perfect.
